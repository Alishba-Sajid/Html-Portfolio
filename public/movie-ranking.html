The Best Movies According to Alishba

My top 3 movies of all time

Tangled

The short film takes place shortly after the events of the 2010 Disney animated film Tangled. It depicts the wedding ceremony of the film's main characters, Rapunzel and Eugene (also known as Flynn Rider).

The story begins with the kingdom preparing for Rapunzel and Eugene's wedding. The couple's pet chameleon Pascal and the horse Maximus are enlisted to be the ring bearers for the ceremony. However, the rings are accidentally lost and the two animals have to go on a comedic chase through the kingdom to try and find them before the wedding can take place.

The short features several slapstick and humorous moments as Pascal and Maximus cause chaos trying to locate the lost rings. In the end, they are able to retrieve the rings just in time for the couple to complete their wedding vows and officially become husband and wife.

The short highlights the loving relationship between Rapunzel and Eugene, as well as the entertaining dynamic between the supporting animal characters Pascal and Maximus. It provides a lighthearted follow-up to the events of the original Tangled film.

Beauty and The Beast

The story centers around a young woman named Belle who lives in a small provincial town. Belle is seen as an outcast in the town due to her love of reading and independent spirit.

One day, Belle's father gets lost in the woods and seeks shelter in an enchanted castle, owned by a cursed prince who has been transformed into a hideous Beast. The Beast imprisons Belle's father, but Belle offers to take his place as the Beast's prisoner.

Belle begins to see past the Beast's frightening exterior and the two slowly develop a friendship. The Beast, who was once cruel and selfish, starts to change due to Belle's kindness and compassion. Meanwhile, a vain and arrogant townsman named Gaston tries to force Belle to marry him.

As Belle and the Beast's bond grows stronger, a magical spell can only be broken if the Beast learns to love another and earn their love in return before the last petal falls from an enchanted rose. After a series of adventures and challenges, Belle confesses her love to the Beast, breaking the spell and transforming him back into a handsome prince.

The story explores themes of inner beauty, the transformative power of love, and overcoming prejudices. It has become one of the most beloved and iconic fairy tales of all time.

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

The story centers around a young boy named Charlie Bucket, who lives in poverty with his parents and four grandparents. Charlie's life changes when the eccentric chocolatier Willy Wonka announces a contest - five golden tickets have been hidden in Wonka chocolate bars, and the finders will be given a tour of Wonka's mysterious chocolate factory and a lifetime supply of chocolate.

As the golden ticket winners are revealed, we meet a cast of colorful characters - the gluttonous Augustus Gloop, the gum-chewing Violet Beauregarde, the spoiled Veruca Salt, and the television-obsessed Mike Teavee. Meanwhile, Charlie is desperate to find a golden ticket, but his family is too poor to afford the chocolate.

After a series of mishaps and disqualifications of the other ticket winners, Charlie finally finds the last golden ticket. Charlie and his Grandpa Joe join the other children on the factory tour, where they witness the unique and imaginative production processes Wonka has in place.

In the end, Charlie proves himself to be the most deserving winner, displaying honesty, kindness, and moral character. Wonka rewards him by declaring Charlie the heir to the Wonka chocolate empire.
